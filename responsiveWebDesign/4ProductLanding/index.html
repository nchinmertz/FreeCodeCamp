About

The Try Guys is an American comdey group who prodcues content for their streamer, 2nd Try, as well as their YouTube channel. The group was orginally frounded in 2014 by Eugene Lee Yang, Keith Habersberger, Ned Fulmer, and Zach Kornfeld while working at Buzzfeed. They left to start their own independent production company in 2018, 2nd Try LLC. In 2024 they expanded the cast after the departure of Yang and Fulmer.
